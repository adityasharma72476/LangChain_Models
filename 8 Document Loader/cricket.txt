# The Spirit of Cricket

Cricket is more than just a game,
It is a sport of honor and fame.
With bat and ball beneath the sky,
Every player aims to fly high.

The bowler runs with perfect pace,
A smile of focus on the face.
The batter waits with steady eyes,
Dreaming of shots that touch the skies.

The crowd erupts with every four,
And cheers even louder for one run more.
A six that sails beyond the ground,
Creates the sweetest victory sound.

Fielders dive with all their might,
Turning hard moments into delight.
A brilliant catch, a perfect throw,
Shows the skill that players know.

Victory teaches joy and pride,
Defeat builds strength from deep inside.
Every match tells a brand-new story,
Of courage, teamwork, and lasting glory.

From village fields to stadium lights,
Cricket creates unforgettable sights.
Young and old together stand,
United by bat, ball, and cheering fans.

Legends inspire every generation,
With dedication, discipline, and determination.
Their records may one day be broken,
But their spirit will always be spoken.

Cricket teaches patience and grace,
Respect for every time and place.
Win or lose, the lesson is clear,
Play with honesty, never fear.

So whenever the match begins,
Every heart believes it wins.
Because cricket is not just about the score,
It is a passion forevermore.
